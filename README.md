# HR-Analytics-Employee-Retention

Objective
Analyze employee data to identify factors influencing attrition and retention.

Tools Used
Power BI | Excel | Tableau| Data Analysis | Data Visualization

Key KPIs
Overall Attrition Rate
Department-wise Attrition
Average Monthly Income
Average Working Years
Employee Satisfaction
Work-Life Balance

Key Insights
Some common insights obtained from the analysis include:

Employees working overtime show higher attrition rates.
Employees with lower job satisfaction are more likely to leave.
Early-career employees have higher turnover.
Certain departments experience higher attrition than others.
Employees with lower salaries are more likely to resign.
Better work-life balance is associated with improved employee retention.
Longer-tenured employees generally have lower attrition.
